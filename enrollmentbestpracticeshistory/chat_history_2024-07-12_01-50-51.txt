User: What are online student best practices?

Bot: Online students can benefit greatly from a range of support services that are tailored to their specific needs. Some best practices for online students include:

1. Ensuring 24/7 technical support for quick resolution of any issues with the Learning Management System (LMS), software, or access.
2. Providing access to digital libraries, research databases, e-books, and academic journal articles to help students complete assignments effectively.
3. Offering virtual tutoring services and remote academic advising sessions for guidance on course selection, study strategies, and academic goals.
4. Prioritizing accessibility services to accommodate students with screen reader capabilities, captioning, and alternative exam formats.
5. Creating an interactive learning environment with multimedia elements and online simulations to enhance engagement.
6. Facilitating communication and regular interaction among students and instructors through discussion forums, check-in videos, and messaging platforms.
7. Building peer support networks or study groups to help students collaborate, share resources, and support each other academically and socially.
8. Organizing virtual events, guest lectures, or webinars on topics of interest to engage students and help them feel part of the institutional community.
