User: how do i take pto

Bot: To take PTO, you can use the Insperity Portal. Here are the steps to follow:
1. From the left navigation menu, select ‘Time and Attendance’.
2. Select ‘Requests’.
3. Click the blue button that says “New Request.”
4. Select the blue circular button with a white “+” on it to add a date to the request.
5. Choose the correct date and the exact number of hours you’ll be taking off.
6. From the ‘Pay Type’ dropdown list, choose the relevant reason for your time off.
7. Click the orange “Add Dates” button.
8. Repeat steps 4 - 7 for each day you’re requesting.
9. Review dates for accuracy and update the scheduling field for any partial day requests.
10. Click the orange “Submit Request” button.

If you need to cancel your PTO request, you can follow these steps:
1. From the left navigation menu, select ‘Time and Attendance’.
2. Under ‘Daily Procedures’ on the left, select ‘Requests’.
3. Scroll down to see all PTO requests and choose the one you want to cancel.
4. Select ’Cancel Request’.

Remember to always check with your manager for guidance on navigating the unlimited PTO plan and ensure that your PTO hours are added correctly to the system.
