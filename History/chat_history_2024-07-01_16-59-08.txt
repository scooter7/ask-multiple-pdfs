Bot: To take PTO, you can follow these steps:

2. Click the blue button that says “New Request.”
3. Select the blue circular button with a white “+” on it to add a date to the request. Choose the correct date and the exact number of hours you’ll be taking off.
4. From the “Pay Type” dropdown list, choose the relevant reason for your time off.
5. Click the orange “Add Dates” button.
6. Review dates for accuracy and update the scheduling field for any partial day requests.
7. Click the orange “Submit Request” button.

If you need to cancel your PTO request, you can do so through the desktop app by following these steps: 

1. From the left navigation menu, select ‘Time and Attendance’ and then select ‘Requests.’
2. Scroll down to see all PTO requests and choose the one you want to cancel.
3. Select ’Cancel Request’.

Remember to update your personal calendar once the cancellation has been made.
