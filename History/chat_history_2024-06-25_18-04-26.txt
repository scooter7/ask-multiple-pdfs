User: How do I submit a PTO request

Bot: To submit a PTO request, follow these steps:
1. Use the TimeStar system on your computer or the mobile app.
2. Select the dates and hours you are requesting off.
3. Choose the reason for your time off from the "Pay Type" dropdown list.
4. Press submit to send a notification to your manager for review and approval.
5. Repeat these steps for each day you are requesting off.
6. Review the dates for accuracy and update any partial day requests.
7. Click the orange "Submit Request" button to finalize your PTO request.
